"Demodogs, offspring of the Demogorgon, are introduced in "Stranger Things" Season 2. Resembling smaller versions of the Demogorgon with a canine appearance, they are faster, more agile, and hunt in packs. Controlled by the Mind Flayer, a powerful entity from the Upside Down, they pose a significant threat to Hawkins, Indiana, in the protagonists' efforts to thwart the Mind Flayer's invasion."

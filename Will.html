"Will Byers, played by Noah Schnapp, is a key character in "Stranger Things." He's a quiet and artistic boy in Hawkins, Indiana, who mysteriously vanishes early in the series, sparking a town-wide search that uncovers supernatural secrets. Despite his limited screen time in Season 1 due to his disappearance into the Upside Down, Will's integral to the plot as his friends and family strive to find him. In subsequent seasons, Will grapples with the trauma of his experience, including visions and supernatural connections, while remaining resilient and vital to his friends' adventures."

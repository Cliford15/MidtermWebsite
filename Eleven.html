Description:

"Eleven, also known as "El," is a key character in "Stranger Things," portrayed by Millie Bobby Brown. She is a young girl with psychokinetic and telepathic powers, developed from experiments conducted on her mother during pregnancy. Eleven escapes from the government lab where she was held and tested, and is found by Mike Wheeler and his friends in Hawkins, Indiana. Throughout the series, she uses her abilities to help her friends tackle supernatural threats from the Upside Down, a parallel dimension. Eleven's journey is defined by her deep connections with her friends, her growing understanding of the world, and her fight for her own identity and freedom."

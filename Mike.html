Description:

"Mike Wheeler, portrayed by Finn Wolfhard in "Stranger Things," is a central Character from Hawkins, Indiana. He emerges as the leader of his friend group, the "Party," and serves as the Dungeon Master in their Dungeons & Dragons games, which reflect the supernatural challenges they face. Mike's life changes when he meets Eleven, a girl with psychokinetic powers, during the search for his missing friend, Will Byers. He becomes Eleven's protector and close friend, integrating her into his group and hiding her from authorities. Characterized by his strong moral compass and deep loyalty, Mike's relationship with Eleven and his commitment to his friends are central to the series, highlighting themes of courage, friendship, and young love."

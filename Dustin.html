"Dustin Henderson, played by Gaten Matarazzo, is a key character in "Stranger Things," part of the tight-knit group in Hawkins, Indiana. Known for his curly hair, trucker hats, and distinctive lips, Dustin stands out with his intelligence, humor, and tech savvy. He's a loyal friend, often providing insights and solutions to the group's supernatural challenges. Dustin shares a special bond with Steve Harrington, evolving from acquaintances to brothers in arms, battling the Upside Down together. His character brings both humor and heart to the series, earning him a devoted fan following."

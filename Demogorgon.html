"The Demogorgon, is a key antagonist in "Stranger Things," originates from the parallel dimension known as the Upside Down. With its humanoid appearance and ability to traverse interdimensional portals, it preys on residents of Hawkins, Indiana, including Will Byers in Season 1. The creature's actions drive much of the plot as the characters, led by Mike Wheeler's group of friends, strive to rescue will and uncover the town's mysteries.
